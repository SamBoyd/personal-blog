# Essay Title

> Tip: Make the title specific. It should hint at your argument, not just restate the topic.

---

## Introduction

<!-- 
GOAL:
- Introduce the topic
- Narrow the focus
- Present a clear thesis with 3 supporting points
-->

### Hook
<!-- 
1–2 sentences that introduce the topic.
Can be:
- A surprising observation
- A short anecdote
- A broad claim
- A question (sparingly)
-->

Write hook here.

### Context
<!-- 
2–4 sentences narrowing the topic.
Explain:
- Why this topic matters
- What debate/problem exists
- What angle you're focusing on
-->

Write context here.

### Thesis Statement
<!-- 
One clear sentence that:
- Takes a position
- Is arguable
- Includes 3 supporting points (these will become body paragraphs)

Structure idea:
"In this essay, I argue that X because A, B, and C."
-->

Write thesis here.

<!--

3. Evidence/example
4. Analysis
5. Link back to thesis
-->

### Topic Sentence
<!-- 
State the first reason from your thesis.

-->

Write topic sentence here.

### Explanation
<!--

-->

### Evidence / Example
<!--

<!--

# ✏️ Revision Checklist

Before submitting:

- [ ] Does the introduction clearly present an arguable thesis?
- [ ] Does each body paragraph focus on ONE main idea?
- [ ] Does each paragraph clearly connect back to the thesis?
- [ ] Is there enough explanation after each example?
- [ ] Have I removed unnecessary words?
- [ ] Have I read it aloud for clarity?
- [ ] Did I avoid introducing new ideas in the conclusion?
